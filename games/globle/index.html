How to Play Globle

Objective

Guess the mystery country in as few attempts as possible.

Color Hints

Countries are colored based on distance to the mystery country. Dark red = very close, light colors = far away.

Making Guesses

Type any country name and click "Guess" or press Enter. Use autocomplete suggestions to help.

Winning

Find the correct country to win! Start a new round to play again with a different mystery country.
