Protection Tips

Recommendations to improve your privacy and reduce tracking

Use Privacy-Focused Browsers

Consider using browsers like Firefox with privacy settings enabled, or Tor Browser for maximum anonymity.

Browser Extensions

Install privacy extensions like uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, or Canvas Blocker to prevent fingerprinting.

Adjust Browser Settings

Disable JavaScript when possible, block third-party cookies, and use private/incognito mode.

Use VPN Services

A VPN can help mask your IP address and location, reducing one aspect of your digital fingerprint.
